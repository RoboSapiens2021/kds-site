Validating concepts

Few entrepreneurs know which formulation will be the right one or “click” in the market. You may need to test the market with a variation of the same formulation or even different product formulations. You need to test in a small scale to conserve capital and/or to diversify your investments. KDS Group can produce as low as 500 bottles as a special customized test run for you. You can validate the concept from the market with real time revenue. We can help you to get to market…fast!
